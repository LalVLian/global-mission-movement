Our Vision

At GMFM, USA, our vision is clear: to raise and send global mission forces that will expand God's kingdom across every corner of the earth. Inspired by the teachings of Matthew 24:14 and Mark 16:15, we aim to empower believers to share the love and message of Jesus Christ with all people.

Our Mission

Our mission is guided by five core purposes: fulfilling the Great (Matthew 28:18-20), partnering with local and global missions, skills for effective outreach, those in need as Christ's ambassadors, and training leaders to inspire and mentor others in their faith journeys.

Core Values

We glorify God, seek the Holy Spirit's guidance, uphold His Word, and demonstrate love through action. We make faith-based decisions, serve others, communicate truth with love, maintain professional quality, ensure transparency, foster unity, and prioritize prayer in all initiatives to fulfill our mission in Christ.
